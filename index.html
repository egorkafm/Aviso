    Brazil has a massive copyright problem. Everybody thinks downloading of movies on the internet is legal and doesn’t harm anybody. But it does.

    If Brazilian distributors are not able to sell the movies they purchase from producers, they will not generate a Portuguese dub, you will only see fewer and fewer movies in cinemas.

    If producers are not able to sell their movies to local distributors, they will stop to produce movies or limit the budget so that the quality gets lower. No big explosions, not crashed cars.

    Legislation

    Why do we send out notices?

    We want to give downloaders that maybe didn’t know that it is illegal to download the chance to late purchase the movie / settle the claim as economic as possible at this stage. The costs necessary to identify and your misbehavior are limited to investigation and a portion of the initial court proceedings. Therefor you are offered to pay the movie price plus an extra charge for downloading it instead of buying it.

    How we got your information?

    Your computer or device is connected to the internet using a digital address. An Investigator detected your Internet Address (IP) and captured evidence that your household is in possession and makes available of a movie that has been downloaded from the internet. The Rightsholder filed a lawsuit against your internet service provider to receive your personal information. We will provide you with the court order so that you can see that this is perfectly legal.

    What happens if I don’t pay the penalty?

    We are instructed by the Movie Studio to go the distance and sue individuals that stole the movie instead of purchasing it. If you don’t use the opportunity.

    Why do I have to pay more than the movie costs in stores?

    Although we are trying to be as fair as possible at this stage, you are still the reason that the rightsholder had costs. (to hire an investigator and pay for legal expenses). This is still a penalty and it will be cheaper in future to just buy the movie. It doesn’t get cheaper than that.
